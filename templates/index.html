Free Software Jobs

This site is under construction and will host a portal for Free and Open-Source Software (FOSS) jobs, see https://github.com/hjacobs/freesoftwarejobs.org

Why?

I felt the personal need for a job portal for Free Software jobs as one of my main criteria for any future job is the ability to work with and on FOSS. The FSF webpage has only a tiny section on "Free Software Jobs" and most existing job portals are not specialized in Free Software / FOSS (they sometimes have tags such as "open source", but without a clear criteria).

What is a "Free Software Job"?

A "Free Software Job" in the context of this site is any job where at least 50% of the time contribute to Free and Open-Source Software (FOSS). Contribution does not necessarily involve writing software --- job profiles such as UX, product management, QA and translation for FOSS are equally important. Working *with* FOSS alone is not enough to satisfy this criteria, i.e. a Linux administration job in an otherwise proprietary environment does not fit.

Is a fee involved for posting a job offer?

No. I believe there should be no barriers for posting Free Software Jobs. The FSF charges a fee and I suppose that's one reason their page lists only very few jobs.
